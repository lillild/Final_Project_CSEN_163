Easy Veggie Fried Rice

✓ Rice cooker ✓ Stovetop & pan

Uses from your pantry: rice, egg, onion

You’d also need: oil,peas, carrot, soy sauce

How to make it

  1. Break up leftover rice so the grains are separate. Heat oil in a hot pan and toast half the rice until lightly browned and chewy. Transfer and repeat.
  2. Push rice to the sides. Add oil, then cook onion and carrot until fragrant. Toss with the rice and stir in soy sauce.
  3. Move rice aside and scramble eggs in the center. Season, cook until just set, then mix into the rice.
  4. Add frozen peas and stir until hot and every grain of rice is separate. Serve immediately.
